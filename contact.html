Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the typical response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Premium support customers receive priority response times.

How do I get technical support for an MCP server?

For official MCP servers, email support@mcphubz.com with details about your issue. For community servers, we recommend reaching out on our community forum.

Can I request a custom MCP implementation?

Yes! Contact our consulting team at business@mcphubz.com with your requirements, and we'll get back to you with a proposal.

How do I report a bug or security issue?

Please report bugs to support@mcphubz.com. For security issues, please use security@mcphubz.com for responsible disclosure.
