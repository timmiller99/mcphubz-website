Marketplace FAQ

Common questions about buying and selling on the MCPHubz Marketplace

How do I purchase an item?

To purchase an item, simply click the "View Details" button on any listing, then click "Purchase" on the item page. You'll need to create an account if you don't already have one.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin and Ethereum.

How do I become a seller?

To become a seller, create an account, then click "Become a Seller" in your account dashboard. You'll need to provide some additional information and agree to our seller terms.

What commission does MCPHubz take?

MCPHubz takes a 10-15% commission on sales, depending on the seller's volume and history. The exact commission rate will be displayed in your seller dashboard.

How are payments processed?

Payments are held in escrow for 14 days to ensure buyer satisfaction, then released to the seller. Sellers can withdraw funds via bank transfer, PayPal, or cryptocurrency.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team at support@mcphubz.com for assistance with returns or refunds.
